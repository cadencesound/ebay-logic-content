FEATURE

Woofer Cone

The Logic LPW series slim subwoofers use a black special Polypropylene cone with an oversized butyl rubber surround.

Slim Mount Design

The Logics LPW series slim subwoofers are designed and engineered with a much smaller motor assembly which reduce the overall depth of the woofer allowing it to fit in slim enclosures and tighter locations of the vehicle.

Terminals

The Logic LPW series slim subwoofers feature two pair of heavy-duty spring post terminals which can accept single or dual (standard 3/4" width) banana plugs, pin connectors, or bare wire up to 10-gauge.

Seal Box

Logics recommends a sealed enclosure with an internal volume 1.0 ft³ (28.32 liters. Sealed enclosures provide the most control over the woofer, will handle a lot of power, and provide accurate sonic reproduction, making them suitable to all types of music.

Ported Box

The recommended ported box volume is 1.2 ft³ (33.98 liters), using a single 4" (101.60 mm) diameter x 11" (279.40 mm) long port. Ported (or vented) enclosures typically provide better overall efficiency than sealed, maximizing bass output for a given amplifier.
