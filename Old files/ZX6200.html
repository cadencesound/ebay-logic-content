FEATURES

Cone

The Logic ZX series speakers feature black chrome plated injection cones.

Surrounds

Moisture-resistant black glossy butyl rubber surround that does not only offers a wider frequency response but also higher SPL.

Tweeters

Each speaker uses a 1/2" Green Chrome PEI (Poly-Ether-Imide) neodymium dome tweeter for crisp highs.

Mounting

The speaker basket has multiple mounting holes and is designed to fit a standard 3-hole 6-1/2" application, Ford's 4-hole 6-1/2" application, and a standard 4-hole 6-3/4" application. (When mounting in a 6-3/4" application, use a speaker baffle to seal the inner ring of screw holes.)
