Cone

The Logic LPH series 2-way pro audio horn series feature high density black paper cones.

Surrounds

The Logic LPH series 2-way pro audio horn series use a treated cloth accordion surround that withstands extreme excursion when the volume dial is turned up, provides both stability and extended long-term use as well.

Spider

The Logic LPH 2-way pro audio horn series use conex spider that ensures reliability and helps avoid interference typical of many high-excursion designs.
