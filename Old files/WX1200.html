FEATURE

Woofer Cone

The Logic WX series subwoofers use top grade poly mica coated paper cone with a high-quality stitched butyl rubber surround.

Dual 4 Ohm Voice Coil

The Logic WX series subwoofers feature two 4 ohms voice coils, offering wiring flexibility. When the voice coils are wired in parallel, the woofer presents a 2 ohms load; when wired in series, the woofer presents an 8 ohms load.

Terminals

The Logic WX series subwoofers feature two pair of heavy-duty spring post terminals which can accept single or dual (standard 3/4" width) banana plugs, pin connectors, or bare wire up to 10-gauge.

Sealed Box

Logics recommends a sealed enclosure with an internal volume 1.0 ft³ (28.32 liters). Sealed enclosures provide the most control over the woofer, will handle a lot of power, and provide accurate sonic reproduction, making them suitable to all types of music.

Ported Box

The recommended ported box volume is 1.2 ft³ (33.98 liters), using a single 4" (101.60 mm) diameter x 11" (279.40 mm) long port. Ported (or vented) enclosures typically provide better overall efficiency than sealed, maximizing bass output for a given amplifier.
