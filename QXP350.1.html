LOGIC SOUNDLAB QXP350.1 – 1000W Monoblock Car Amplifier | Class D MOSFET Power Amp

The LOGIC SOUNDLAB QXP350.1 is a high-efficiency Class D monoblock amplifier designed for those who demand powerful bass without taking up excess space. Featuring 1000W peak power and advanced MOSFET circuitry, this amp delivers clean, distortion-free audio while maximizing power efficiency. Perfect for installations where space matters but performance is key.

Why We Love It?

⚡ Powerful & Space-Saving – 1000W Peak Power with 350W RMS @ 1 Ohm in a sleek design.

⚡ High-Efficiency Class D – Ensures low heat output & power-saving performance.

⚡ MOSFET Switching Power Supply – Provides clean, stable, and reliable power to your subwoofers.

⚡ Customizable Sound – Adjustable low pass filter (50Hz-250Hz) & bass boost (0-6dB).

⚡ Built-in Protection – Speaker short, thermal, and overload protection for added safety.

⚡ LED Indicators – Power and protection status at a glance.

Technial Specification

General Infomation:

  • Peak Power: 1000W x 1 @ 1 Ohm
  • RMS Power: 350W x 1 @ 1 Ohm
  • THD @ RMS Power: < 0.2%
  • Channel Separation: > 45dB
  • Signal-to-Noise Ratio: 100dB

Design & Connectivity:

  • Class D Amplifier for high efficiency
  • PWM Controlled DC to DC Switching Power Supply
  • RCA Line In / Line Out Preamp
  • LED Power & Protection Indicators
  • Fuse Rating: 40A x 1
  • Recommended Wiring: 8-gauge power and ground leads (not included)
  • Dimensions: 7.2” (W) x 2.3” (H) x 11.9” (L)

Adjustable Controls:

  • Low Pass Filter: 50Hz – 250Hz
  • Bass Boost: 0 – 6dB
