DBX3400 | 3400W Monoblock Class D MOSFET Power Amplifier

Unleash the Power of Your Car Audio System! Upgrade your vehicle’s audio performance with the Logic Soundlab DBX3400, a high-power 3400-watt monoblock amplifier designed for deep, distortion-free bass.
